## Context
BAFTA award-winning screenwriter and stand-up comedian. One of the most prominent British-Pakistani writers working in Hollywood TV right now.

**Career path:** Data scientist → domestic violence support worker → stand-up comedy → screenwriting. Started in comedy under the name Bisha Kanval Ali. Participated in Royal Court Theatre's Young Writers programme (2012). Selected for BAFTA's Elevate programme (2018).

**Major credits:**
- **Ms. Marvel** (Disney+, 2022) — Head Writer & Showrunner. First Muslim superhero in the MCU. Won Gracie + Golden Tomato Award.
- **Black Mirror** S6 (Netflix, 2023) — Executive Producer of the full series + co-wrote "Demon 79" with Charlie Brooker. Won **BAFTA Award for Best Writer: Drama**.
- **Loki** (Disney+, 2021) — Writer + Executive Story Editor
- **Four Weddings and a Funeral** (Hulu, 2019) — Writer (with Mindy Kaling)
- **Sex Education** (Netflix) — Additional material
- **The Baby** (HBO/Sky) — Consultant Producer

**Other work:**
- Stand-up comedian — co-presented The Guilty Feminist podcast with Deborah Frances-White
- GrownUpLand for BBC Radio 4
- Written for The Huffington Post
- Established a Netflix/Sky fellowship scheme for underrepresented creatives in film & TV

**Representation:** Casarotto Ramsay & Associates
